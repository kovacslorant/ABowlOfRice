Kung Pao Lajos's restaurant...

An authentic restaurant straight from Japan. The recipes of the famous Michelin-starred chef Lajos Kung Pao are given a modern look at the dishes of feudal Japan. They guide us on a culinary journey. The style of the time is mixed with the modern way of thinking, which was implemented under the personal supervision of Lajos when designing the restaurant. It's not just a meal, it's a trip straight back to medieval Japan, a memorable experience for the whole family.


The Japanese are renowned for their precision and tradition, which can be seen in the dishes they prepare with great care and attention. Cooking for them, like many other things, is an art. Due to their island characteristics and isolation, their food is very exotic. These are the dishes that will end up on your table.

Taste of Japan

Original recipes from Japanese, a thousand-year-old tradition of food preparation combined with the expectations of the modern age. Traditional dishes that provide an unforgettable experience. Our chefs brought their knowledge straight from the island of Japan, which is passed down from generation to generation. Careful selection of ingredients, which give our dishes premium quality and unadulterated taste both from foreign and Hungarian producers.
